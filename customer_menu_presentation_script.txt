PART 1: INTRODUCING THE CUSTOMER MENU & QR ENTRY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN ACTION]
Show the index.html page or a printed QR code. Then open the Customer Menu page
by simulating a QR scan (or opening "menu.html?table=5" in the browser address bar).

[PRESENTATION SCRIPT - ENGLISH]
"Good morning/afternoon respected examiners. Today, I am presenting the Customer 
Menu module of the Athugalpura Restaurant SmartServe system. 

In a traditional restaurant, customers have to wait for a waiter to get a physical 
menu. In our SmartServe system, the customer simply scans a table-specific QR code 
placed on their table. 

As you can see, when I scan the QR code for Table 5, the system automatically redirects 
us to the customer menu page with the Table Number '5' locked and active in the header. 
This ensures that the orders placed from this device are automatically routed to Table 5 
without any manual entry errors by the customer or wait staff."

[සිංහල පැහැදිලි කිරීම / SINGLISH GUIDANCE]

- මෙතනදී supervisor ලට පෙන්වන්න: "මේ බලන්න table number එක header එකේ display වෙනවා,

PART 3: CART MANAGEMENT & PLACING AN ORDER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN ACTION]
Click on a food item card (e.g., "Spicy Chicken Burger"). Increase the quantity to 2, 
type a note (e.g., "No onions, extra spicy"), and click "Add to Cart". 
Open the shopping cart panel by clicking the floating cart icon on the bottom right. 
Type a Customer Name (e.g., "Saman") and click "Place Order".

[PRESENTATION SCRIPT - ENGLISH]
"Let's simulate placing an order. I will select the 'Spicy Chicken Burger'. A popup modal 
appears allowing me to customize my order. I will set the quantity to two and add a 
special cooking instruction: 'No onions, extra spicy'. 

Now, I will add it to the cart. If I open the floating shopping cart at the bottom 
right, we can see the order summary, the calculated subtotal, and our special instructions. 

To submit the order, the customer can enter their name so the staff can identify them. 
I will type 'Saman' and click 'Place Order'. The ordering system now triggers a 
real-time broadcast to the Kitchen Display System and the Waiter Dashboard using 
Supabase WebSockets. No page refresh was needed."

[සිංහල පැහැදිලි කිරීම / SINGLISH GUIDANCE]

- Cart icon එක click කරලා cart slide-out panel එක පෙන්වන්න.

PART 4: UNDER THE HOOD - REAL-TIME TECHNOLOGY (FOR HIGH MARKS!)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN ACTION]
Explain this verbally or show the browser console if they ask, referring to the system 
architecture.

[PRESENTATION SCRIPT - ENGLISH]
"Under the hood, this application uses a unique serverless architecture. We do not 
run a local Apache PHP server to handle requests. Instead, we have developed a custom 
'supabase_helper.js' script. 

This script intercepts standard AJAX fetch requests made by the frontend, translates 
them into Secure PostgreSQL queries, and communicates directly with Supabase Cloud Services. 
For real-time notifications, we use Supabase Realtime Channels. 

So the moment our customer Saman clicked 'Place Order', a row was written to the 
'orders' and 'order_items' tables, and a WebSocket event instantly broadcasted this to 
the kitchen display."

[සිංහල පැහැදිලි කිරීම / SINGLISH GUIDANCE]
- මේක serverless client-side architecture එකක්. 
- frontend එක කෙලින්ම Supabase cloud database එකත් එක්ක connect වෙන්නේ `supabase_helper.js` එකෙන් 
  fetch request intercept කිරීමෙන්.
- Real-time updates සදහා Supabase WebSockets (Realtime Channels) භාවිතා වෙනවා.

They can click on the 'History' tab on their menu.

When the chef in the kitchen starts preparing the food on the Kitchen Display System,

PART 6: PAYMENT OPTIONS (ONLINE vs. PHYSICAL BILL)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SCREEN ACTION]
Show the two buttons in the active order/cart panel: "Pay Online" and "Bring Physical Bill". 
Click "Bring Physical Bill" and explain what happens.

[PRESENTATION SCRIPT - ENGLISH]
"Finally, when the customer is ready to check out, we provide two convenient payment pathways:

First, is 'Pay Online'. This directs them to a secure mock online payment portal. Once 
completed, the order status in Supabase is updated to 'Paid' instantly, free-ing up the 
table session.

Second, is 'Bring Physical Bill'. For customers paying with physical cash or card, clicking 
this button immediately triggers a visual and sound alert on the Waiter's dashboard. 
The waiter will see that Table 5 requested a physical bill, bring the paper bill to 
their table, collect the money, and settle the session on the Waiter Dashboard.

This completes the customer-side lifecycle. It minimizes human waiting time, makes the 
ordering experience frictionless, and ensures high accuracy."

[සිංහල පැහැදිලි කිරීම / SINGLISH GUIDANCE]
- payment methods දෙකක් තියෙනවා: 'Pay Online' (Card checkouts) සහ 'Bring Physical Bill' (Cash).
- 'Bring Physical Bill' click කරාම waiter.html එකට live audio/visual notification එකක් 
  යනවා "Table 5 requested bill" කියලා.
- Waiter ඇවිල්ලා සල්ලි අරන් waiter dashboard එකෙන් table session එක settle කරාම table 
  එක ආයෙත් free වෙනවා.
