================================================================================
                      RESTAURANT QR ORDERING SYSTEM
                               USER MANUAL
================================================================================

Welcome to the Restaurant QR Ordering System! This system makes it easy for 
customers to order food from their tables, and helps waiters, kitchen staff, 
and admins manage orders and payments smoothly.

This manual explains how to use all 4 parts of the system:
1. Customer Menu & Ordering (menu.html)
2. Waiter Dashboard (waiter.html)
3. Kitchen Display (kitchen.html)
4. Admin Dashboard (admin.html)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. CUSTOMER MENU & ORDERING (menu.html)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This is the screen the customers see on their mobile phones when they scan 
the QR code on their table.

* How to Access:
  - Scan the QR code printed on your table. It automatically opens the menu 
    with your Table Number active.

* How to Order Food:
  1. Browse food categories (Appetizers, Mains, Drinks, Desserts).
  2. Click on a food item to open details, select quantity, and add to Cart.
  3. Click the Cart button (bottom right) to see your items.
  4. Type your name (optional) and click "Place Order".
  5. Your order is sent straight to the Kitchen!

* How to Pay for your Food:
  After placing orders, you can pay using two options from the Cart or Active Orders panel:
  
  A. ONLINE PAYMENT:
     - Click the "Pay Online" button.
     - Choose your payment type (Card, Mobile Pay, etc.) and complete the payment.
     - The system immediately marks your order/session as "Paid".
     
  B. PHYSICAL PAYMENT (CASH / CARD TO WAITER):
     - Click the "Bring Physical Bill" button.
     - A notification is sent to the Waiter immediately.
     - The waiter will bring the paper bill to your table, collect your cash or 
       swipe your card, and mark your table as resolved.

* How to View Order History:
  - Click the "History" or "Order History" button in the menu.
  - You can see all the items you ordered during your current visit, their 
    prices, and their real-time cooking status (Pending, Preparing, Ready).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. WAITER DASHBOARD (waiter.html)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This dashboard is used by waiters on tablets or phones to monitor tables and 
assist customers.

* Live Notifications (Left Side):
  - Waiters see live alerts with sound.
  - "New Order Placed": Shows which table ordered and what they ordered.
  - "Call Waiter": Customer needs help at a specific table.
  - "Bill Requested": Customer wants a physical bill brought to their table.
  - Action: Click "Dismiss" once you attend to the table.

* Table Grid (Right Side):
  - Shows all tables in the restaurant (e.g., Table 1 to 20).
  - Colors show table status:
    * Blue/Active: Table currently has customers ordering.
    * Grey/Free: Table is empty.
  - Clicking on an Active Table opens the Bill Details:
    * You can see the list of all items they ordered.
    * Click "Close Session & Settle Cash" to mark their pending physical bills 
      as Paid and reset the table to "Free" for the next customers.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. KITCHEN DISPLAY (kitchen.html)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This screen is placed on a screen in the kitchen. Chefs use it to see new 
orders and update their preparation progress.

* View Incoming Orders:
  - Orders appear in order cards from oldest to newest.
  - Each card shows: Table Number, Order ID, Items and Quantities, and Elapsed Time.
  - Cards play a sound when a new order arrives.

* Updating Order Status:
  - Click "Start Preparing" (yellow button) when you start cooking. 
    (Customer sees status update: "Preparing").
  - Click "Mark Ready" (green button) when the food is cooked. 
    (Customer sees status update: "Ready", and Waiter gets a alert to serve).
  - Click "Dismiss/Served" once the food is taken to the customer table.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. ADMIN DASHBOARD (admin.html)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Used by managers and owners to control the menu, staff, tables, and view reports.

* Dashboard Overview:
  - Shows live statistics: Today's Revenue, Active Orders, Occupied Tables, 
    and Popular Dishes.

* Menu Management (Menu Tab):
  - Add New Food Items: Enter name, category, price, description, and photo.
  - Edit/Delete Items: Modify existing prices, availability, or photos.

* Tables & QR (Tables Tab):
  - Add or delete tables.
  - Generate and download QR codes for new tables.

* Staff & Assignments (Staff / Assignments Tabs):
  - Add staff accounts (Waiters, Chefs, Admins).
  - Assign specific waiters to manage specific tables.

* Reports & Analytics (Reports Tab):
  - Select date ranges to see sales reports.
  - Download reports as Excel/CSV or print sales summaries.
